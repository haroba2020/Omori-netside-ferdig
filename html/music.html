Omori's Music

The soundtrack of OMORI was produced by the OMORI Sound Team, encompassing Pedro Silva (SLIME GIRLS), Jami Lynne (CLOVER & SEALIFE) and bo en. It features songs from multiple genres ranging from retro to orchestral. Many of the songs are characterized by various snippets of sound effects.

PEDRO SILVA is a composer from Los Angeles California. They've previously contributed to video game projects such as OOBLETS, Guildlings and Button City.

JAMI LYNNE (Heads Up! Hot Dogs, Wonder Momo: Typhoon Booster) is a composer and performing musician from Tucson, AZ. Jami blends electronic pop music with the emotional imperfection of punk rock and silver-age idol music to create an experience that pushes forward into the future with the ideals of youth and hope.
